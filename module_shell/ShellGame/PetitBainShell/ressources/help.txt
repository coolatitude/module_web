This is what you learned thus far:

'back' : Will bring you back to Artemis in front of the main gate.

'executables' : Executables are programs that can be used with './'. 
They are in blue.

'directories' : Directories contain executables, sub-directories and much more files.
They are in green.

'./' : It allows you to execute an executable. In this case it allows you to interact with characters and objects.
